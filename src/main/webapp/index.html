The Golden rules of the Library

  • Library Access
    • The library is accessible only to registered students, faculty, and staff of the college.
    • External visitors or guests must obtain permission from the librarian or administration to access library resources.
  • Library Timings
    • The library is open from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays and 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM on weekends.
    • The library remains closed on public holidays and during college events unless otherwise notified.
  • Library Membership
    • Students must register for a library card at the start of the academic year.
    • Loss of the library card should be reported immediately. A replacement card will be issued with a nominal fee.
  • Borrowing Rules
    • Students can borrow up to 3 books at a time for a maximum period of 14 days.
    • Reference books, journals, and periodicals cannot be borrowed and must be used within S the library premises.
